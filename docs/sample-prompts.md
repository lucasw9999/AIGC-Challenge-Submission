## Sample Prompt 1 — Shot 04: The First Note

*The emotional fulcrum of the film. The moment Lucas's bow touches the string is the moment memories begin.*

**Prompt:**

Medium shot, eye-level, 50mm-equivalent lens, inside a warm wood-paneled community room at the Sunrise at FlatIrons senior-living facility. In the foreground, a calm Asian-American teenage boy, age 14, with warm olive skin, neatly cut short dark-black hair, a quiet serious expression, wearing a crisp white button-down shirt with sleeves rolled once, black slim slacks, and black polished shoes, is seated on a simple wooden chair in three-quarter profile to the camera; his violin is tucked cleanly under his chin and the horsehair bow is just drawing the first note across the outermost string in a sustained slow continuous motion over the full eight seconds; the string vibrates visibly, a tiny glow of resin dust rises at the contact point. In the soft-focus background, a semicircle of six elderly residents in cream-upholstered armchairs — silver and grey heads in gentle silhouette, faces softly out of focus, one elder leaning slightly forward. A large mullioned window on camera-left pours golden late-afternoon sun across the wooden floor, painting a warm rectangle. Time 4:30 PM late spring. Lighting: warm 2900K directional sunlight from the window at a 30-degree angle, soft cream bounce fill from the rear wall, rim light on silver hair in the background, golden dust motes drifting in the beam. Camera performs a very slow 2% dolly push-in toward the bow's contact point on the string across the full eight seconds. Palette: cream, honey-wood, soft silver, warm white, deep amber. Mood: reverent, attentive, a room holding its breath as the song begins. photorealistic cinematic realism, live-action film quality, warm naturalistic color grade, shallow depth of field, authentic human expressions and real human skin, natural lighting, subtle film grain, Chloé Zhao aesthetic

**Negative prompt:**

crisp audience faces, distorted hands, violin held incorrectly, bow held like a pencil, modern phones or devices, visible wristwatch, animated, cartoon, anime, painted, watercolor, oil painting, ink-wash, illustration style, CGI look, plastic skin, uncanny valley faces, distorted fingers, six-fingered hands, modern logos, text artifacts, cluttered background, harsh contrast

## Sample Prompt 2 — Shot 09: A Kind Stranger in a Chinese Market

*Shows how dialogue was embedded directly into the prompt — Google Veo 3.1 generated lip-synced Mandarin speech alongside the visuals.*

**Prompt:**

Medium close-up, eye-level at child height, 50mm-equivalent lens, same crowded Chinese wet market setting. A kind adult Chinese woman, age approximately 35, with soft olive skin, warm shoulder-length dark hair, a gentle sympathetic face with faint kind smile lines, wearing a simple light-blue cotton blouse and loose dark-grey trousers with a simple canvas shopping bag slung over her shoulder, has stopped and kneels down gently on one knee beside a young Asian boy, age approximately 6, with warm olive skin, short black hair, wearing a plain white cotton t-shirt and dark navy shorts; the boy looks slightly unsure with softly shiny eyes. Across the full eight sustained seconds she kneels gently, her right hand softly resting on the boy's small shoulder in a reassuring grounding touch, her expression warm and concerned; she asks gently in Mandarin: "Xiǎo péngyǒu, nǐ mí lù le ma?" (meaning "Little friend, are you lost?"). The boy looks up at her, his small face lifting uncertainly, and he slowly nods in quiet acknowledgment. Market stalls blur in soft focus behind her. Time is 10 AM summer morning. Lighting: warm 4500K dappled market awning light, gentle warm bounce onto their faces. Camera performs a slow 2% dolly push-in over the full eight seconds. Audio: the woman's warm Mandarin voice clearly saying "Xiǎo péngyǒu, nǐ mí lù le ma?"; soft market ambient continuing behind; the boy's soft quiet breath. Palette: light blue, warm olive skin, navy, soft green and red market background. Mood: a kind adult becomes a moment of shelter. photorealistic cinematic realism, live-action film quality, warm naturalistic color grade, shallow depth of field, authentic human expressions and real human skin, natural lighting, subtle film grain, Chloé Zhao aesthetic, slightly softer memory grade with faint warm haze suggesting recollection

**Negative prompt:**

elderly grandmother, creepy old man, food-offering, Western-feature adult or child, stern face, painted red tear streaks, animated, cartoon, anime, painted, watercolor, oil painting, ink-wash, illustration style, CGI look, plastic skin, uncanny valley faces, distorted fingers, six-fingered hands, modern logos, text artifacts, harsh contrast

---

**Why these two:**

- Shot 04 is the visual and emotional center of the film — the bow touching the string is the moment memories begin. It shows the sustained-moment prompt style, and the cinematic detail I specified (lens, Kelvin lighting, dust motes, camera push magnitude).
- Shot 09 shows how dialogue was embedded directly into the prompt — the Mandarin line is written into the prompt text, and Google Veo 3.1 generated lip-synced Mandarin speech alongside the visuals. This is the kind of AI capability I wanted to demonstrate.

Model used for all shots: **Google Veo 3.1** (`veo-3.1-generate-001`) at 1080p / 8 seconds / 16:9.
